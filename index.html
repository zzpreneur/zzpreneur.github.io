    Hi, I'm Zach Zuber.
    I like to build things.

    I live by a simple motto; get things done and have fun.

    What I bring to the table

    Details matter, from a bird's-eye view to a magnifying glass inspection. I focus on improving peoples lives by building tools that work. Whether that means jumping into a rails app to fix a bug, sketching out a new screen, or picking up the phone to talk with a customer, I tinker, listen, and observe to find the best solution.

    I believe a great product simplifies peoples lives. Makes people smile. Relates to people. Takes care of people. And gets out of the way. Joining a team that believes in this too and wants to build great products is where I want to be.
