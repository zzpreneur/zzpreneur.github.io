Adding value or features

I am a better writer because of Basecamp's text documents. I wanted to improve them. I had cool ideas. Like auto saving, hints on sentence structure, and improving readability. But they were not improving users' capability of sharing, collaborating and saving notes, so I decided to stop.

It's better to know when you're adding value and when you're adding features. I want to share the lessons learned from this experience. View the full image below by clicking on it. Scroll down for insights into my process.

Concept

Just looking at the image above your thinking, "What the hell man you just changed the background from yellow to white." That's true, but there is more.

Basecamp promotes good writing skills. I wanted to help make it's users better writers too. Stealing thunder from the Hemingway app I wanted to implement hints on sentence structure.

Changing the background from yellow to white made the page breath better. This gives the writer an area where their focus is on the words their typing.
